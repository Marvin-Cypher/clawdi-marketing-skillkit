# Workflows You Can Automate with an AI Chief of Staff

![workflows-you-can-automate-with-an-ai-chief-of-staff cover](../imgs/workflows-you-can-automate-with-an-ai-chief-of-staff/cover-google.png)

- <header.jpg> Blog post: Artificial intelligence is increasingly moving beyond generating text and into managing real workflows. Instead of handling tasks manually across multiple tools, many professionals are beginning to rely on AI assistants to automate routine operations.

Here are seven practical workflows that can be automated with an AI chief of staff.

## 1. Daily Inbox Triage

Every morning, many people spend time reviewing emails to determine which messages require attention.

An AI assistant can scan incoming emails, prioritize urgent messages, summarize long threads, and suggest replies.

This allows users to focus only on the most important conversations.

## 2. Meeting Coordination

Scheduling meetings often involves multiple messages and calendar checks.

An AI assistant can automatically review calendar availability, suggest meeting times, send invitations, and update schedules.

This removes much of the manual coordination.

## 3. Daily Founder Brief

Founders and operators often need a quick overview of what is happening across their business. An AI assistant can compile a daily briefing that includes important emails, upcoming meetings, key updates, and reminders.

This provides a clear snapshot of the day ahead.

## 4. Document Summaries

Long documents and reports can take significant time to review. AI assistants can summarize research reports, internal documents, meeting notes, and project updates.

This helps teams absorb information more quickly.

## 5. Weekly Status Reports

Many teams spend time preparing weekly updates. An AI assistant can gather information from multiple sources and automatically generate a structured report.

This saves time while keeping everyone informed.

## 6. Research and Information Gathering

AI assistants can also help with research tasks by browsing the web, summarizing sources, and compiling findings.

This is useful for preparing presentations, reports, or strategic decisions.

## 7. Workflow Automation Across Apps

One of the most powerful capabilities of AI assistants is connecting different tools.

For example, an AI assistant might extract information from an email, create a task in a project management tool, schedule a meeting, and send a summary to the team. By linking multiple apps together, these systems can automate entire workflows.
